Transfer Tips

  • Never transfer speculatively — find availability first, then transfer points
  • Transfer times matter — award seats can disappear. KrisFlyer & Asia Miles are fastest
  • ANA requires round-trip bookings — but the pricing is per round-trip, making it incredible value
  • Hold if possible — some programs let you hold an award ticket for 24-72hrs while points transfer
  • Taxes vary wildly — SQ charges low taxes; BA/QF charge fuel surcharges that can be $500+ per person

Recommended Approach

1

Search Now (Today)

Aug 2026 is ~4.5 months away. Some airlines release award space 330-355 days out, so availability should be opening up. Start searching immediately on:

  • Seats.aero — set up alerts for your route/dates
  • Qantas.com — search Classic Rewards for Qatar QSuites
  • ANA website — check round-trip availability (best value)
  • KrisFlyer — SQ own flights to Milan
2

Be Flexible on Dates & Airports

Search a window of Aug 25 – Sep 2. Consider:

  • Venice (VCE) — ideal, Qatar & Emirates fly here direct from their hubs
  • Milan (MXP) — 2.5hr train to Venice, opens up SQ & Cathay options
  • Rome (FCO) — more availability, 3.5hr train or cheap domestic flight
3

Mix & Match Outbound/Return

You don't need the same airline both ways. Great combos:

  • Out: Qatar QSuites via DOH → Return: SQ via SIN
  • Out: Emirates First via DXB → Return: ANA via TYO
  • Out: SQ Suites via SIN → Return: Cathay via HKG
4

Don't Transfer Until Ready

Keep points in Amex MR until you've confirmed availability with 2 seats. Then:

  1. Confirm 2 seats are available on the exact flights
  2. If the program offers a hold, place a hold
  3. Transfer the exact points needed (transfers are one-way & irreversible)
  4. Book immediately when points arrive
5

Watch for These Opportunities

  • Amex transfer bonuses — periodically 20-30% bonus to certain partners (makes expensive options cheaper)
  • Last-minute award dumps — airlines often release more award space 2-4 weeks before departure
  • Mixed cabin — some programs allow First on one leg, Business on another
