Transfer Tips

  • Never transfer speculatively — find availability first, then transfer points
  • Transfer times matter — award seats can disappear. KrisFlyer & Asia Miles are fastest
  • ANA requires round-trip bookings — but the pricing is per round-trip, making it incredible value
  • Hold if possible — some programs let you hold an award ticket for 24-72hrs while points transfer
  • Taxes vary wildly — SQ charges low taxes; BA/QF charge fuel surcharges that can be $500+ per person

Recommended Approach

1

Use Live Search First

The Live Search tab queries Seats.aero for real award availability across multiple programs. Search your dates and save any good options.

2

Be Flexible on Dates & Airports

Search Aug 25 – Sep 5. Consider:

  • Venice (VCE) — ideal, Qatar & Emirates fly here direct from their hubs
  • Milan (MXP) — 2.5hr train to Venice, opens up SQ & Cathay options
  • Rome (FCO) — more availability, 3.5hr train or cheap domestic flight
3

Mix & Match Outbound/Return

You don't need the same airline both ways. Use "Search Return" to find return flights separately.

4

Don't Transfer Until Ready

Keep points in Amex MR until you've confirmed 2 seats. Transfers are one-way & irreversible.

5

Check What Seats.aero Doesn't Cover

Seats.aero may not track all routes. Use the Airline Links tab to manually check:

  • ANA — round-trip pricing (88k Biz RT!) not reflected in Seats.aero per-leg pricing
  • Qantas.com — best for Qatar QSuites availability on oneworld
  • KrisFlyer — SQ's own flights often only bookable on their site
